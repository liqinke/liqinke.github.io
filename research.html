1 Research

Recent research interests:

  • Develop and apply ab initio dynamic spin susceptibility to describe magnetic interactions and spin excitations [npj Comput Mater 7, 4 (2021)].
  • Develop a highly efficient Green’s-function-based ab initio framework to analyze exchange coupling and pairwise magnetocrystalline anisotropy [Phys. Rev. B 99, 054418 (2019)].
  • Besides numerical methods, I also enjoy analytical modeling, especially modeling of spin-orbit-coupling physics in non-rare-earth [Phys. Rev. B, 92:014423 (2015)] and rare-earth systems [arXiv:2201.11265].
